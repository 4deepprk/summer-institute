Overview

We provide state-of-the art training in a range of different areas in computational social science from ethics to text analysis and mass collaboration. Below you can find archives of all of the videos, slides, code, and teaching exercises used in 2019. These lectures assume a basic, working knowledge of the R language. If you do not yet know R, this resource is a great place to start. If you are a teacher, the source code for all of our teaching materials is available here.
